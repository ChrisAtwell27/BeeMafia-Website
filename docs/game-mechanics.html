Game Phases

Each game of BeeMafia progresses through multiple phases that repeat until a team wins.

üåÖ

Setup Phase

Duration: 30 seconds

At the start of the game, each player is secretly assigned a role. During this phase:

  • Players receive their role card with abilities and win condition
  • Players can read and understand their role
  • No actions can be performed yet
  • Prepare your strategy based on your role
üåô

Night Phase

Duration: 60 seconds

The night is when most roles perform their special abilities:

  • Players with night actions submit their targets
  • Wasp team members can communicate in secret chat
  • Dead players can talk in dead chat
  • Actions are processed at the end of the phase
  • Results are revealed at the start of the next day
Tip: Submit your action early! You can change it before the phase ends.
‚òÄÔ∏è

Day Phase

Duration: 180 seconds

During the day, all living players can discuss what happened:

  • Night results are announced (who died and how)
  • Players share information and suspicions
  • Discuss who should be voted out
  • Form alliances and coordinate strategies
  • Watch for suspicious behavior or claims
Tip: Pay attention to what players say and who they accuse!
üó≥Ô∏è

Voting Phase

Duration: 120 seconds

Time to decide who gets eliminated:

  • Click on a player's name to vote for them
  • You can change your vote before the phase ends
  • The player with the most votes is lynched
  • Ties result in no elimination
  • Lynched players reveal their role and die
Tip: Vote based on evidence and discussion, not random guesses!

Attack and Defense System

BeeMafia uses a tiered attack and defense system. If an attack's power is greater than the target's defense, the target dies.

üó°Ô∏è Attack Levels

0
No Attack

Cannot kill players

1
Basic Attack

Standard killing power (Wasp kills, Soldier Bee)

2
Powerful Attack

Strong attacks (Veteran Bee, Murder Hornet)

3
Unstoppable Attack

Cannot be blocked (Fire Ant ignite)

üõ°Ô∏è Defense Levels

0
No Defense

Vulnerable to all attacks

1
Basic Defense

Blocks basic attacks (Nurse Bee heal)

2
Powerful Defense

Blocks basic and powerful attacks (Butterfly vest)

3
Invincible Defense

Blocks all attacks except unstoppable

How it works: If attack power > defense level, the target dies. For example:
  • Basic Attack (1) vs No Defense (0) = Target dies
  • Basic Attack (1) vs Basic Defense (1) = Target survives
  • Powerful Attack (2) vs Basic Defense (1) = Target dies
  • Unstoppable Attack (3) vs Powerful Defense (2) = Target dies

Chat System

Communication is key in BeeMafia. Different chat channels are available based on your role and status:

üí¨ All Chat

Available to all living players during Day Phase

  • Discuss night results and suspicions
  • Share investigation results (or lies!)
  • Coordinate voting decisions
  • Visible to everyone who is alive

üêù Wasp Chat

Private chat for Wasp team members at night

  • Only Wasp team can see and send messages
  • Coordinate who to kill
  • Share investigation results from Spy Wasp
  • Plan deception strategies
  • Spy Bee can read this chat!

üëª Dead Chat

Available to all dead players

  • Watch the game unfold
  • Discuss what happened
  • Living players cannot see this chat
  • Medium Bee can communicate with dead

Win Conditions

Different teams and roles have different ways to achieve victory:

üêù Bee Team Victory

Eliminate all Wasps and harmful Neutrals

The Bee team wins when there are no more threats remaining. This includes:

  • All Wasp team members eliminated
  • All Neutral Killing roles eliminated (Murder Hornet, Fire Ant, etc.)
  • All Neutral Evil roles eliminated (except Spider, who can side with Bees)
  • Neutral Benign roles can survive with Bees

üêù Wasp Team Victory

Equal or outnumber all other players

Wasps win through elimination and deception:

  • When Wasps ‚â• (Bees + Neutrals)
  • No hostile Neutrals that can kill Wasps remain
  • Voting becomes impossible for non-Wasps
  • Wasps can kill faster than they can be eliminated

‚öñÔ∏è Neutral Win Conditions

Varies by role

  • Murder Hornet / Fire Ant: Be the last player alive
  • Clown Beetle: Get lynched during voting
  • Bounty Hunter: Get your target lynched
  • Spider: Survive to see Bees or Wasps lose
  • Butterfly / Gossip Beetle: Survive until game ends
  • Pirate Beetle: Win 2 duels
  • Guardian Ant: Your target survives and wins
  • Matchmaker Beetle: Your linked partner wins
  • Amnesiac: Remember a role then achieve that win condition
  • Mercenary: Your assigned team wins

Strategy Tips

üêù As a Bee

  • Share your investigation results to find evils
  • Protect confirmed Bee roles
  • Watch for contradictions in claims
  • Vote together to eliminate threats
  • Don't reveal your role unless necessary

üêù As a Wasp

  • Coordinate kills in private chat
  • Claim a believable Bee role
  • Accuse others to deflect suspicion
  • Target investigators and protectors first
  • Support your teammates' claims

‚öñÔ∏è As a Neutral

  • Keep your true role hidden
  • Play to your specific win condition
  • Can claim to be Bee or Wasp as needed
  • Kingmaker role in close games
  • Adapt strategy based on game state

üéØ General Tips

  • Pay attention to voting patterns
  • Keep notes on player claims and actions
  • Use the process of elimination
  • Read the chat carefully for slips
  • Balance aggression with caution

Action Priority

When multiple actions happen to the same player, they resolve in this order:

  1. Transport: Swaps happen first, redirecting all other actions
  2. Jail: Jailed targets are protected and roleblocked
  3. Witch Control: Changes target's action target
  4. Roleblock: Prevents target's action
  5. Protection: Heals and guards apply
  6. Investigation: Info gathering actions
  7. Attacks: Killing actions resolve
  8. Deception: Disguises and cleans apply after death
